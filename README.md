# The Championship Cube: A Front Office Framework for Building NBA Champions 🏀
Target Conference: MIT Sloan Sports Analytics Conference 2027

## Overview
The Championship Cube is a six-dimensional analytical framework designed to identify the statistical and organisational factors that consistently separate NBA title winners from contenders. Rather than analysing championship success through talent alone, this framework examines six interdependent faces — each representing a distinct dimension of championship construction.

## The Six Faces

Team Face — Era-adjusted roster construction and team statistics
Player Face — Individual contribution, role fit and acquisition method
Coach Face — System building, culture and coach-roster fit
Front Office Face — Draft philosophy, trade strategy and free agency
Controllable External Face — Load management, scheduling and sports science
Uncontrollable External Face — Injuries, variance and resilience

All data is sourced from Basketball Reference and covers every NBA champion and Finals runner-up from 1990 to 2026. The dataset is era-adjusted to allow fair cross-era comparison.
